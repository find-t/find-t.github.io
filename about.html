About
TMIS is the prototype for a format-combusting architectural practice still in the process of defining itself. Born at ETH Zurich, based in Oslo and Zurich.

Our claim is simple: provide new tools for the 3d-scanning of construction waste to assess its reuse potential, effectively providing solutions for the decarbonization of the architecture industry.

Please note that this website is under construction. Full deployment is scheduled for july 2025.
