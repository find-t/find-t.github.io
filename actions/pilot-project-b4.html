About

The former Museum of Contemporary in Oslo has been closed and disused for several years. No plan for its re-use has been developed, and none is foreseeable in the near future. TMIS has conducted an intensive 3d scanning of the structure to index and categorize the scanned elements to analyse and determine their aptitude for reuse, effectively testing the deployment potential of our services.
